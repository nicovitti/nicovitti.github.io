ABOUT

Nico Vitti is a designer, photographer, and activist who lives in Baltimore, MD. He has loads of passion for graphic design and years of experience in photography. He has a B.A. in American Studies from Wesleyan University in Middletown, Connecticut and in 2014 he will graduate from Maryland Institute College of Art with an M.A. in Social Design.
